Episode Guide

Season 1

  • More than Meets the Eye, Part 1 - September 17, 1984
    The Autobots and Decepticons crash on Earth and begin their battle.
  • More than Meets the Eye, Part 2 - September 18, 1984
    The Autobots form alliances with humans, while the Decepticons plan to return to Cybertron.
  • More than Meets the Eye, Part 3 - September 19, 1984
    The Autobots thwart the Decepticons' plans, but the battle continues.
  • Transport to Oblivion - October 6, 1984
    Megatron plans to use a space bridge to transport energy to Cybertron.
  • Roll for It - October 13, 1984
    The Decepticons steal an experimental anti-matter formula.

Season 2

  • Autobot Spike - September 23, 1985
    Spike's mind is transferred into Autobot X, causing chaos.
  • The Immobilizer - September 24, 1985
    Wheeljack's invention falls into Decepticon hands.
  • Dinobot Island, Part 1 - September 25, 1985
    The Autobots discover an island of living dinosaurs.
  • Dinobot Island, Part 2 - September 26, 1985
    The Decepticons exploit the island's energy resources, leading to a battle.
  • Traitor - September 27, 1985
    Mirage is suspected of betraying the Autobots to the Decepticons.
