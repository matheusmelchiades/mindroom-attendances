Optimus Prime

The noble leader of the Autobots who transforms into a fire truck. Optimus Prime is known for his courage and sense of justice.

Bulkhead

One Robot Wrecking Crew. Enough size and strength to bust up Decepticon armies, but is very gentle and kind at heart.

Prowl

One of Optimus Prime's most trusted Autobots and military strategist.

Bumblebee

Small but tough Autobot that has an affinity for interacting with humans. He is one of the most well known Autobots and in most incarnations is Optimus Prime's right hand man. The latest incarnations of this character has had a inability too speak and having to rely between hand signs and/or radio channels to make whole sentences.
