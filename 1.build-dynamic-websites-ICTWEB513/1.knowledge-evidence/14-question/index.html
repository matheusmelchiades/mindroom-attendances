Did You Know?

  • In the original series, the Autobots and Decepticons were originally designed to transform into household items and weapons.
  • Transformers has inspired numerous comic books, animated series, movies, and toy lines since its debut in the 1980s.
  • Optimus Prime's iconic line "Autobots, roll out!" has become synonymous with the franchise.
